Why do we do this?

We created this website to provide clear and accessible information about the different types of energy used today and to raise awareness about excessive energy consumption in households, especially the often unnoticed “phantom consumption” from devices that remain plugged in. This issue not only increases electricity bills but also negatively impacts the environment by raising overall energy demand. To address this problem, we developed a device designed to reduce this unnecessary consumption, combining awareness with a practical solution to promote more efficient and responsible energy use.
